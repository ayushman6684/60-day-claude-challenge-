## 🧠 Learnings

- **ATS systems scan for exact keywords** — skills you use daily but don't mention = invisible to ATS
- **Communication gaps ≠ skill gaps**: Most missing items were already part of my work; I just hadn't written them explicitly
- **Summary section has high ATS weight** — front-loading keywords here boosts match score significantly
- **Formatting matters**: ATS-friendly resumes avoid tables, columns, and graphics — plain structured text wins
- **Targeting each application**: A single generic resume rarely scores above 80%; tailoring to each JD is key
- **Metrics + Keywords = Maximum impact**: Quantified achievements + relevant keywords is the winning formula
