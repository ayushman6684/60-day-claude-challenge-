# Day 22 – Startup Validation Report

## #60DayClaudeChallenge

### Project

**InternTrust – AI-Powered Paid Internship Platform**

A verified internship platform that helps students showcase their skills through practical assessments and interviews rather than relying solely on resumes.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often face:

* Fake internship listings
* Registration fee scams
* Lack of interview opportunities
* Resume-based filtering
* Limited ways to demonstrate practical skills

Companies face:

* Difficulty screening candidates
* Too many low-quality applications
* Limited hiring resources

---

## Proposed Solution

InternTrust provides:

* Verified companies only
* Zero registration fees for students
* Interview-based selection
* Skill-based assessments
* AI-powered candidate matching
* Scam-free internship opportunities

---

## Key Assumption Challenged

### Assumption

> If thousands of students join the platform, companies will automatically come.

### Reality

This assumption was challenged.

Students are the primary users, but companies are the actual customers and the more difficult side of the marketplace to acquire.

### Key Learning

Building trust and value for employers is essential before scaling student acquisition.

---

## Go / No-Go Recommendation

### Recommendation: GO ✅

Conditions:

* Conduct startup interviews
* Validate employer demand
* Build waitlist
* Test with real internship placements

---

## What I Learned

* Validate assumptions before building
* Trust can be a stronger differentiator than AI
* Marketplace businesses require balancing both sides
* Customer discovery is critical
* Real problems matter more than features
