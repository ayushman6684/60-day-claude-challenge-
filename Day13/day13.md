\# Day 13: AI-Powered Job Search Assistant 🚀



\## Overview

Built an AI-powered job search assistant by connecting Claude to the \*\*Indeed connector\*\*, enabling live job discovery, fit analysis, and market insights based on my professional profile.



\## Workflow

1\. Provided Claude with my professional profile (background, skills, experience, location, goals)

2\. Defined job search criteria (target roles, locations, salary expectations, exclusions)

3\. Asked Claude to search live job postings via the Indeed connector and analyze fit



\## Results

\### Market Insights

\- \*\*2x pay gap\*\* between top two matches (₹25k vs ₹50k per month)

\- \*\*Remote roles dominate\*\* the top matches

\- \*\*Spring Boot + React\*\* combination is a rare differentiator in the current market

\- \*\*Overall fit score:\*\* 7/10



\## Key Learnings

\- Connecting Claude to job platforms (via connectors) transforms it from a chatbot into an active job-search agent

\- AI can surface non-obvious market signals (pay gaps, skill differentiators) that aren't visible from a single job listing

\- Skill-gap analysis helps prioritize what to learn next (e.g., React alongside Spring Boot) to stand out

\- Real-time job matching + scoring saves significant manual search time
