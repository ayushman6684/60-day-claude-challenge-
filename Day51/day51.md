# Day 51 – Capstone Kickoff 🚀

## #60DayClaudeChallenge

### Project Chosen

For my 10-day capstone, I chose to build **CrackIt – AI Resume Defense Simulator**.

CrackIt is an AI-powered interview preparation platform that goes beyond traditional mock interviews. Users can upload or paste their resume, provide a target job description, and face an AI interviewer that challenges every claim on the resume with realistic technical and behavioral questions.

The platform is designed primarily for college students and freshers preparing for internships and entry-level software engineering roles.

---

# Problem Statement

Many candidates know their resume well but struggle to confidently defend their projects, technical skills, and achievements during interviews.

Existing interview platforms usually ask generic questions instead of questions based on the candidate's actual resume and target role.

CrackIt solves this by creating personalized AI-driven interview sessions tailored to both the resume and the job description.

---

# Biggest Scoping Decision

The biggest decision was to **focus on building a polished MVP instead of trying to build every possible feature.**

Rather than implementing authentication, user dashboards, analytics, and voice conversations, I prioritized the features that deliver the highest value within the 10-day timeline.

### MVP Features

- Resume Upload (PDF/DOCX)
- Resume Paste Option
- Job Description Input
- AI Resume Analysis
- Adaptive Interview Questions
- Friendly / Standard / Tough Interview Modes
- Structured Interview Scorecard
- AI Feedback
- Suggested Better Answers
- Downloadable PDF Interview Report
- Responsive Modern UI
- Free-tier Deployment
