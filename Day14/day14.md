\# Day 14: AI Red Flag Detector 🚩



\## Overview

Built an AI-powered job description analyzer using Claude that detects red flags, 

toxic workplace signals, and hiring risks — so job seekers don't get burned later.



\## The Prompt Used



You are an AI Red Flag Detector for job seekers.

Analyze the Job Description and Company Information.

Identify:

1\. Unrealistic Requirements

2\. Toxic Workplace Signals

3\. Remote Job Authenticity

4\. Hiring Risks

5\. Company Risks



Output:

\- Overall Risk Score (0-100)

\- Top Red Flags with severity (1-10)

\- Positive Signals

\- Risk Breakdown Table

\- Final Verdict (Apply / Caution / Avoid)

\- 5 Smart Interview Questions



\## Risk Analysis Report



\### Role Analyzed: Junior Full Stack Developer (Remote)



\### Overall Risk Score: 90/100 — EXTREME RISK ⚠️



\### Top Red Flags



| Red Flag | Severity |

|----------|----------|

| Unrealistic Requirements — "Junior" role asking 5+ years \& too many tech stacks | 10/10 |

| Burnout \& Toxic Culture — "Work hard, play hard", "24/7 support", "fast-paced" | 9/10 |

| Remote Job Authenticity — Relocation to Bengaluru may be required after probation | 8/10 |

| Hiring Transparency — No salary range, vague responsibilities, "family" culture | 8/10 |

| Company Stability — Only 8 months old, no funding info, rapid hiring, long hours reported | 7/10 |



\### Positive Signals

\### Final Verdict

❌ AVOID — Too many red flags for a "Junior" role.

High risk of burnout, unrealistic expectations \& potential instability.



> Your career is a marathon, not a sprint. 🏃



\## 5 Smart Interview Questions

1\. Can you clarify why this role is labeled "Junior" but requires 5+ years of experience?

2\. What does success look like in the first 3, 6, and 12 months?

3\. How often is 24/7 support actually expected and how is it managed?

4\. Is relocation to Bengaluru mandatory or optional? When is it expected?

5\. What is the current team structure and how is work distributed?



\## Key Learnings

\- Job titles can be misleading — always cross-check role label vs. actual requirements

\- "Family culture" and "wear many hats" are often red flags, not perks

\- Remote roles with hidden relocation clauses are a common trap for freshers

\- AI can analyze JDs in seconds and surface risks that are easy to miss manually

\- Asking smart questions in interviews protects your time and mental health

\- A high risk score doesn't mean never apply — it means go in with eyes open
