Basics

What's a SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan lets you put a fixed amount into a mutual fund every month, automatically — turning small, regular saving into long-term growth via compounding.

Basics

Mutual funds, simply

A mutual fund pools money from many investors to buy a mix of stocks or bonds, managed by professionals — an easier entry point than picking stocks yourself.

Basics

Risk & time horizon

Longer time horizons can absorb more short-term ups and downs. As a student, time is your biggest advantage — even small amounts compound meaningfully over a decade.
