# Day 16 – Build Your First Stock Research Skill

## Challenge
Build a custom Claude Skill that can analyze stocks using fundamentals, financial statements, valuation metrics, ownership trends, profitability ratios, and business quality.

---

## Skill Details

### Skill Name
stock-fundamental-research

### Description
Analyze Indian and global listed companies using fundamentals, financial statements, business quality, competitive advantages, valuation, risks, and growth prospects. Generate evidence-based research reports and investor-friendly summaries. Never provide direct buy, sell, or hold recommendations.

---

## What I Built

I created a custom Claude Skill called **stock-fundamental-research**.

The skill can:

- Generate Quick Take stock reports
- Perform Deep Dive fundamental analysis
- Compare two stocks side-by-side
- Analyze strengths and risks
- Review ownership patterns
- Evaluate business quality and financial health
- Generate investor-friendly summaries
- Provide valuation analysis and stock charts

---

## Key Learning

One interesting takeaway was that even though TCS and Infosys operate in the same industry, their fundamentals tell different stories.

### TCS
- Strong profitability
- Better cash generation
- Consistent execution
- Higher operational efficiency

### Infosys
- Strong growth potential
- Attractive dividend yield
- Competitive valuation
- Strong consulting capabilities

The biggest learning was that AI becomes much more powerful when combined with a structured framework.

> A good prompt gives answers.
>
> A good system creates analysis.
